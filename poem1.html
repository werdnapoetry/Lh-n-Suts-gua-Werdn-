An Overshoot

Home

I see the lady laughing
with another man,
and realize that I am but a boy without a chance.

I must avert my eyes,
I mustn’t catch a glimpse,
for gazing at this woman now will make my heart go limp.

Her beauty burns into my soul,
which deems the pain impossible,
for understanding that this masterpiece of Jesus does not know

That there’s a man who cares for her, and every evening dreams of her
agonizes me to an extent I thought I’d never know.
